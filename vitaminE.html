Function:

Antioxidant that protects cells from oxidative stress, preventing damage from free radicals. Supports immune health and skin health (helps prevent wrinkles and UV damage). Involved in the protection of red blood cells and the nervous system.

Sources:

Sources: Nuts (almonds, hazelnuts), seeds, vegetable oils (sunflower, olive), spinach, and broccoli.

Deficiency Symptoms:

Can cause nerve damage, muscle weakness, and vision problems. May result in hemolytic anemia, a condition where red blood cells break down too quickly.
