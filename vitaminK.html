Function:

Essential for the synthesis of proteins involved in blood clotting, which help stop bleeding after injuries. Plays a role in bone health by regulating calcium and promoting bone mineralization.

Sources:

Sources: Leafy green vegetables (kale, spinach, broccoli), Brussels sprouts, cabbage, and fermented foods.

Deficiency Symptoms:

Leads to excessive bleeding and bruising. May also increase the risk of bone fractures and osteopenia (low bone mineral density).
