Function:

Critical for calcium absorption, promoting bone health and preventing diseases like osteoporosis. Plays a role in immune modulation, decreasing inflammation, and may have anti-cancer effects.

Sources:

Sources: Sunlight (the body synthesizes it from UV rays). Fatty fish (salmon, sardines, mackerel), fortified dairy products, egg yolks, and mushrooms.

Deficiency Symptoms:

In children, deficiency can lead to rickets, which weakens bones and causes deformities. In adults, leads to osteomalacia (soft bones) and increased risk of fractures.
